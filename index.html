About Scott

Scott Miller is a Piano Tuner/Technician based in West Cumbria, servicing Cumbria and South Scotland. He has been tuning pianos for 14 years, providing an expert piano tuning, maintenance and report service. His clients include theatres, schools, festivals, professional musicians and private players. Scott can advise you on transporting, buying a piano and can check any piano you might be thinking of acquiring.
